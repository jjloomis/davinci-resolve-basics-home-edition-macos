# Adjusting the audio level of all clips in a track

1. Click the **Mixer** tab \(upper-right\) to activate the **Meters**/ **Mixer** panel \(lower right.\)
2. To ensure the **Mixer** is active, click the **More** button \(three dots\) in the **Meters**/ **Mixer** panel. Make sure **Mixer** is selected. 
3. Play your project in the **Timeline**. In the **Mixer** panel, observe the levels of the audio tracks \(**A1**, **A2**, **A3**.\)
4. To adjust the level of an audio track, click and drag the track's fader in the **Mixer**. Adjusting the track's fader will change the audio levels of all clips in the track.
